This chapter will discuss the concept of sexual self. It highlights biological and environmental factors that shape sexual development. It will provide you with suggestions on how to begin the process of redefining your sexual self.

What is Sexual Self?



Sexual self refers to how one thinks about themself as a sexual individual. It speaks of your sexual health, sexual orientation, gender identity, and expression and values around your sexuality. The sexuality or sex of a person is classified as male, female or intersex. A person typically has their sex assigned at birth based on physiological characteristics including their genitalia and chromosome compositions. Gender is a term that refers to social or cultural distinctions associated with a given sex. Gender identity is what is inside us; how we feel about our own gender.
